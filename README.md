# random-stuff-ig
Some random ZIP files, here are readmes for it:
## nmtools-2.0.zip
to use this, you need to first sign your 16-bit tiff heightmap, and then you need to export it as a .raw, oh and also your tiff has to be grayscale which can be done in gimp by going to image>mode and choosing grayscale, and also, you need to remove alpha, this is also easy, just right click on the layer and then click on "Remove Alpha Channel"
## nmtools-signed.zip
nmtools but you don't need to sign your heightmaps, just use nms.u16.exe instead.
